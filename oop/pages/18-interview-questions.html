Common OOP Interview Questions

Frequently asked placement questions with concise answer direction.

▶ High-frequency Questions

  1. What is OOP and why do we use it?
  2. Class vs object?
  3. Encapsulation vs abstraction?
  4. Overloading vs overriding?
  5. Composition vs inheritance?
  6. What is polymorphism with practical example?
  7. What is the diamond problem?
  8. Explain object slicing in C++.
  9. Why virtual destructor?
  10. What are SOLID principles?
  11. What is LSP with a violation example?
  12. When should you use abstract class vs interface?
  13. What is dynamic binding?
  14. What are static members and risks?
  15. How do you design a maintainable class hierarchy?

🌟 Strong-answer template

1) Definition (1-2 lines)
2) Real-world analogy
3) Minimal code example
4) Trade-off / caveat
5) Common interview pitfall

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Object-oriented design should be explained through abstraction boundaries, coupling/cohesion, and maintainability trade-offs.
