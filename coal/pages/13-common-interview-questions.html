Common COAL Interview Questions

Conceptual and computational problems asked in technical interviews.

▶ Conceptual questions

  1. Architecture vs organization: what is the difference?
  2. Why is two's complement preferred over sign-magnitude?
  3. Direct mapped cache vs set associative cache?
  4. What are pipeline hazards and how are they solved?
  5. Why memory hierarchy is necessary?
  6. Interrupt vs DMA in I/O organization?

▶ Small computational problems

  1. Convert (101101.01)2 to decimal.
  2. Represent -29 in 8-bit two's complement.
  3. Compute AMAT for hit time=2ns, miss rate=8%, miss penalty=40ns.
  4. How many lines in direct-mapped cache of 16 KB with 64-byte block?
  5. If CPI without stalls=1 and stall cycles per instruction=0.3, find effective CPI.

🌟 Answer snippets

(101101.01)2 = 45.25
-29 in 8-bit: 00011101 -> invert 11100010 -> +1 = 11100011
AMAT = 2 + 0.08*40 = 5.2 ns
Cache lines = 16*1024 / 64 = 256
Effective CPI = 1 + 0.3 = 1.3
