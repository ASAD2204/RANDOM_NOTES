Number Systems

Binary, decimal, octal, hexadecimal and conversion methods.

▶ Bases and digits

  • Binary (base 2): digits 0-1
  • Octal (base 8): digits 0-7
  • Decimal (base 10): digits 0-9
  • Hex (base 16): digits 0-9, A-F

▶ Conversion examples

(101101)2 = 1*2^5 + 0*2^4 + 1*2^3 + 1*2^2 + 0*2^1 + 1 = (45)10
(93)10 to binary: 93 = 64 + 16 + 8 + 4 + 1 -> (1011101)2
(7A)16 to decimal: 7*16 + 10 = (122)10
(11101101)2 to hex: 1110 1101 -> E D -> (ED)16

🌟 Quick tricks

  • Binary ↔ Octal: group bits in 3.
  • Binary ↔ Hex: group bits in 4.
  • Hex is compact and used for addresses/debugging.
