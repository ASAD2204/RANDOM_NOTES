Sequential Circuits

Output depends on current input and previous state (memory element).

▶ Flip-flops

  • SR Flip-Flop: set/reset storage.
  • D Flip-Flop: stores D at clock edge.
  • JK Flip-Flop: no invalid SR state; supports toggle.
  • T Flip-Flop: toggles when T=1.

▶ Registers and counters

Register is a group of flip-flops storing multi-bit data. Counter advances states on clock pulses (up/down, mod-n).

Clock -> [FF0][FF1][FF2][FF3] -> 4-bit register/counter behavior

🌟 Interview tip

Combinational = no clock/no memory; Sequential = clock + state transition.
