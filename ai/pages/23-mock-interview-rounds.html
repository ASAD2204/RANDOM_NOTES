Mock Interview Rounds (AI Engineering)

Simulated rounds with theory-first answer strategy.

▶ Round 1: Foundations

  • Bias-variance trade-off with practical actions.
  • When to use precision-recall over accuracy?
  • How does leakage invalidate model evaluation?

▶ Round 2: LLM systems

  • Design a RAG assistant for internal docs.
  • How do you evaluate hallucination and grounding quality?
  • RAG vs CAG in multi-turn enterprise chatbot.

▶ Round 3: Production

  • How to roll out a new model version safely?
  • What signals trigger retraining?
  • How to debug latency spikes in inference API?

🌟 Theory focus

Strong interview answers connect model behavior to system constraints: quality, latency, cost, and safety. Explicitly discuss assumptions, trade-offs, and rollback plans.
