▶ Theory Deep Dive

Deployment is where model quality meets real-world constraints: availability, latency, security, and cost. A robust serving stack includes input validation, rate limiting, model version routing, and observability. Mature teams use staged rollout patterns (shadow/canary/blue-green) to reduce deployment risk.

  • Serving contract: stable request/response schema is critical for app integration.
  • Scalability: autoscaling and batching improve throughput under load.
  • Reliability: health checks, retries, and fallback defaults prevent outages.
