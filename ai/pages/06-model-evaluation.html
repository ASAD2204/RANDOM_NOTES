Model Evaluation

How to measure whether a model is accurate, reliable, and useful for decisions.

▶ Classification metrics

  • Accuracy = correct / total
  • Precision = TP/(TP+FP)
  • Recall = TP/(TP+FN)
  • F1 = harmonic mean of precision and recall

Metric choice depends on error cost. In fraud detection, missing fraud (FN) may be worse than false alarms (FP), so recall often gets higher priority.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Evaluation is a policy problem, not just a math problem. A model score becomes a business action only after thresholding, calibration checks, and risk analysis. Offline metrics should be paired with online performance indicators such as latency, cost, and user-impact metrics. Reliable teams define acceptance criteria before training begins.

  • Discrimination: can the model rank positives above negatives?
  • Calibration: do predicted probabilities match actual frequencies?
  • Stability: do metrics hold across segments and time windows?
