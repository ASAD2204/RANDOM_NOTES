Service Company AI Round

Requirement-centric AI delivery and stakeholder communication.

▶ Typical asks

  • How do you scope an AI solution for a client with unclear requirements?
  • How do you estimate cost/latency for an LLM-based product?
  • How do you design fallback behavior for low-confidence outputs?

▶ Theory focus

Service interviews prioritize process maturity. Emphasize requirement clarification, acceptance criteria, baseline metrics, and phase-wise risk control. Show that you can convert business ambiguity into measurable engineering milestones.

🌟 Good answer style

Requirement discovery -> PoC -> baseline metrics -> phased deployment -> SLA-backed monitoring.
