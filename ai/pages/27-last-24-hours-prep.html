Last 24 Hours AI Interview Prep

Final-day plan for high-retention revision and confident interviews.

▶ Final checklist

  • Revise revision packs (19-21) and visual map (22).
  • Practice 6 system-design prompts (RAG, assistant, search, recommender).
  • Practice 5 coding snippets (metrics, retrieval, preprocessing, API, token budget).
  • Prepare one project story: problem, architecture, metrics, trade-offs.
  • Use concise answer structure: assumptions, approach, risks, rollout.

▶ Theory focus

In final prep, prioritize conceptual clarity over memorizing extra details. Interviewers reward structured reasoning and trade-off awareness. Rehearse explaining why a design is chosen, what can fail, and how you would monitor and improve it in production.
