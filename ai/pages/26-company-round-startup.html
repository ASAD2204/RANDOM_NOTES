Startup Company AI Round

MVP-first AI engineering under time and infrastructure constraints.

▶ Typical asks

  • Build an AI feature in 2 weeks: what is your plan?
  • API LLM vs self-hosted model: how do you choose?
  • How do you prioritize reliability under limited budget?

▶ Theory focus

Startup interviews value decision speed with principled trade-offs. Present a phased architecture: fast baseline launch, telemetry collection, then optimization. Show when you accept technical debt and how you contain it with clear migration checkpoints.

🌟 What wins

Clear prioritization, lean architecture, measurable milestones, and practical risk management.
