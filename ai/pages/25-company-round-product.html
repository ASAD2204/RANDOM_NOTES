Product Company AI Round

Scale-oriented interview prep focused on product metrics and architecture trade-offs.

▶ Typical asks

  • Design a recommendation or search ranking system.
  • How do you measure improvement from a new ranking model?
  • How do you reduce hallucinations while preserving helpfulness?

▶ Theory focus

Product interviews reward metric-driven reasoning: define business KPIs, map them to offline/online model metrics, and explain causal risks. Demonstrate understanding of experimentation, counterfactual bias, and reliability trade-offs at scale.

🌟 Interview expectation

Show A/B testing strategy, offline-online gap management, architecture trade-offs, and ownership mindset.
