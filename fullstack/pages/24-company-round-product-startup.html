Product and Startup Full Stack Round

Focus on ownership, product impact, and speed-quality trade-offs.

▶ Product-style asks

  • Design resilient feed/recommendation API with pagination and caching.
  • How do you run A/B tests for frontend changes?
  • How do you reduce p95 latency while preserving reliability?

▶ Startup-style asks

  • Build MVP in 2 weeks: architecture and priorities?
  • Managed services vs self-hosting: your decision factors?
  • How do you prevent tech debt explosion while shipping fast?

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Product/startup rounds emphasize impact-speed trade-offs, architecture pragmatism, and ownership under uncertainty.
