Revision 06-10 (Testing to Interviews)

Fast recap of quality, management, maintenance, and interview framing.

▶ Must-remember points

  • Testing levels: unit, integration, system, acceptance; plus non-functional testing.
  • Project constraints are scope, time, cost, and quality trade-offs.
  • Risk management: identify, assess impact/probability, plan mitigation, monitor.
  • Maintenance types: corrective, adaptive, perfective, preventive.
  • For scenarios, answer with context → options → decision → trade-off.

🌟 Interview structure

Problem clarity + stakeholder impact + execution plan + measurable outcome

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
