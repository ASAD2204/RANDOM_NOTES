Mock Interview Rounds (SE Focus)

Practice realistic rounds with expected answer direction.

▶ Round 1: Fundamentals

  • Explain SDLC with deliverables in each stage.
  • When does Agile fail in practice?
  • Difference between functional and non-functional requirements?

▶ Round 2: Scenarios

  • Production bug under peak traffic: immediate and long-term response?
  • Scope increased without timeline change: how do you renegotiate?
  • Conflicting stakeholder requirements: how do you resolve?

🌟 Evaluation lens

Interviewers check structured thinking, trade-off awareness, and communication clarity.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
