Introduction to Software Engineering

Why software engineering matters and major challenges teams face in real projects.

📚 What is Software Engineering?

Software engineering is the disciplined application of engineering principles to software development, operation, and maintenance to build reliable, scalable, and maintainable systems.

🎯 Why it matters

  • Improves quality and reliability
  • Reduces cost of late defects
  • Enables teamwork and scale
  • Supports long-term maintainability

⚠ Common challenges

  • Changing requirements
  • Time and budget constraints
  • Communication gaps across teams
  • Technical debt accumulation
  • Testing and release pressure

🌟 Interview Tips

  • Define software engineering as process + quality + collaboration.
  • Use examples like banking, e-commerce, or healthcare systems to show why rigor matters.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
