Risk Management

Identify, assess, prioritize, and mitigate risks in software projects.

📚 Risk process

  • Risk identification
  • Risk analysis (probability × impact)
  • Risk prioritization
  • Mitigation planning
  • Monitoring and response

▶ Common software risks

  • Requirement volatility
  • Skill/resource gaps
  • Dependency/vendor delays
  • Security vulnerabilities
  • Performance/scalability bottlenecks

📄 Mitigation examples

Requirement volatility -> iterative delivery + frequent reviews
Skill gap -> training + pairing + hiring buffer
Security risk -> threat modeling + secure coding + pentest
Schedule risk -> phased milestones + contingency buffer

🌟 Interview Tips

  • Use risk register language: risk, probability, impact, owner, mitigation, trigger.
  • Differentiate risk (future possibility) vs issue (current problem).

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
