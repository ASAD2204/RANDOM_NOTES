Service Company Interview Round

Emphasis on process discipline, communication, and delivery reliability.

▶ Typical questions

  • How do you gather and validate client requirements?
  • How do you handle frequent requirement changes?
  • How do you ensure quality in tight deadlines?

▶ Strong response style

Use process-first answers: requirement baseline, change control, test checkpoints, and transparent reporting.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
