Startup Company Interview Round

Emphasis on ownership, speed, pragmatism, and ambiguous problem solving.

▶ Typical questions

  • You are the first engineer for a feature. How do you start?
  • How do you ship MVP fast without creating chaos?
  • How do you prioritize when everything looks urgent?

🌟 What interviewers seek

Clear prioritization, bias to action, pragmatic architecture, and learning loops from production feedback.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
