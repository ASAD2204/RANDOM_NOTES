Revision 01-05 (Intro to Design)

Quick recap of the first half for fast interview revision.

▶ Must-remember points

  • Software engineering applies systematic process to deliver reliable software.
  • SDLC phases: requirements, design, implementation, testing, deployment, maintenance.
  • Waterfall fits stable requirements; Agile suits change and iterative delivery.
  • Requirement qualities: clear, complete, consistent, testable, traceable.
  • Design principles: high cohesion, low coupling, modularity, abstraction.

🌟 60-second interview pitch

“I start from user requirements, pick an SDLC model based on uncertainty,
design modular components, and keep validation through testing and reviews.”

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
