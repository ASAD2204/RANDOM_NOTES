Product Company Interview Round

Emphasis on scalability, reliability, and engineering trade-offs.

▶ Typical questions

  • How would you design release strategy for a high-traffic product?
  • How do you balance velocity with code quality?
  • How do you reduce technical debt without slowing delivery?

🌟 Answer approach

Quantify impact, discuss trade-offs, and show operational readiness (monitoring, rollback, postmortems).

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
