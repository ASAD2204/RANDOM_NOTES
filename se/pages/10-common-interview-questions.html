Common Software Engineering Interview Questions

Theoretical and scenario-based questions with answer direction.

▶ Theory questions

  1. What is software engineering and why is it important?
  2. Explain SDLC phases with deliverables.
  3. Agile vs Waterfall differences.
  4. High cohesion and low coupling meaning?
  5. Unit testing vs integration testing.
  6. What is technical debt?
  7. Corrective vs adaptive maintenance.
  8. What is risk register?

▶ Scenario questions

  1. Requirements keep changing mid-sprint. How do you handle it?
  2. Production bug appears after deployment. What is your response plan?
  3. Project is delayed by 3 weeks. How do you recover?
  4. Testing is incomplete but release date is fixed. What do you do?
  5. Team conflict blocks progress. How do you resolve it?

🌟 Strong answer framework

Context -> Problem -> Options -> Decision -> Trade-off -> Outcome

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
