Software Project Management

Planning, estimation, scheduling, and team coordination essentials.

📚 Core activities

  • Scope definition and planning
  • Effort/time/cost estimation
  • Scheduling and tracking milestones
  • Resource and team management
  • Stakeholder communication

Estimation approaches

  • Expert judgment
  • Analogous estimation
  • Story points (Agile)
  • Function points / COCOMO-style models

📄 Typical artifacts

Project charter
Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
Gantt chart / sprint board
Risk register
Status reports

🌟 Interview Tips

  • Schedule should include buffer for uncertainty and integration risks.
  • Strong managers balance scope, time, cost, and quality (trade-off triangle).

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Software engineering concepts should be framed as process-quality trade-offs across requirements, design, testing, delivery, and maintenance.
