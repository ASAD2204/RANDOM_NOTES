Coding Practice Problems

Beginner to advanced roadmap for technical interviews and quick revision.

▶ Beginner (must solve quickly)

  • Two Sum
  • Valid Parentheses
  • Merge Sorted Arrays
  • Maximum Subarray
  • Reverse Linked List

▶ Intermediate

  • Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters
  • Product of Array Except Self
  • Top K Frequent Elements
  • Number of Islands
  • Coin Change

▶ Advanced

  • Median of Two Sorted Arrays
  • Merge K Sorted Lists
  • Word Break II
  • Alien Dictionary
  • Regular Expression Matching

📄 Practice routine

Daily:
- 2 easy + 2 medium + 1 revision
Weekly:
- 2 timed mock rounds (45-60 min)
Review:
- log mistakes by pattern (graph/DP/window/etc.)

🌟 Final Interview Tips

  • Prioritize pattern mastery over random question count.
  • Re-solve failed problems after 2-3 days without looking.
  • Practice explaining your approach out loud.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Data structure and algorithm choices should be justified by complexity, memory behavior, and workload characteristics.
