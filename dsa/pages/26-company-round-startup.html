Startup-Style DSA Interview

Pragmatic coding, fast iteration, and communication under incomplete constraints.

▶ Typical Questions

  • Implement quickly with reasonable assumptions.
  • Iterate from MVP to optimized version.
  • Handle real-world edge conditions and scaling ideas.

🌟 Winning Approach

  • Say assumptions out loud.
  • Deliver working baseline fast, then improve.
  • Show practical trade-offs instead of theoretical perfection.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Data structure and algorithm choices should be justified by complexity, memory behavior, and workload characteristics.
