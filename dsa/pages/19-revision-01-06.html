Revision Chunk A (01-06)

Complexity, arrays, strings, linked lists, stacks, queues.

📚 10-Minute Recall

  • Complexity first: O(1), O(log n), O(n), O(n log n), O(n²).
  • Arrays: O(1) index, O(n) insert/delete middle.
  • Strings: immutability caveats in Python/JS.
  • Linked list: O(1) local insert/delete with node pointer.
  • Stack LIFO, Queue FIFO core use-cases.
