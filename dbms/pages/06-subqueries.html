Subqueries

Nested and correlated subqueries with examples and outputs.

▶ Nested subquery

-- Employees with salary above company average
SELECT emp_name, salary
FROM employees
WHERE salary > (
  SELECT AVG(salary) FROM employees
);

Output:
Meera 80000
Ravi  75000
Isha  75000

▶ Subquery with IN

SELECT emp_name
FROM employees
WHERE dept_id IN (
  SELECT dept_id FROM departments WHERE dept_name IN ('Engineering','HR')
);

Output:
Asha
Ravi
Meera
Isha

▶ Correlated subquery

-- Employees earning above their department average
SELECT e.emp_name, e.salary, e.dept_id
FROM employees e
WHERE e.salary > (
  SELECT AVG(e2.salary)
  FROM employees e2
  WHERE e2.dept_id = e.dept_id
);

Output:
Meera 80000 10

🌟 Interview Tips

  • Correlated subquery runs per outer row; may be slower than equivalent JOIN in large data.
  • Use EXISTS for existence checks to improve readability/perf.
