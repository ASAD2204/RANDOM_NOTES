Product-Style DBMS Interview

Deeper focus on optimization, indexing strategy, and transaction behavior.

▶ Typical asks

  • Complex joins + correlated subqueries + window functions
  • Explain EXPLAIN plan and optimize query
  • Isolation anomalies and lock behavior
  • Design indexing strategy under read/write trade-off

🌟 Winning Approach

  • Give one correct query + one optimized variant.
  • Explain cost trade-offs with concrete reasoning.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Database engineering decisions should connect schema design, query behavior, indexing, transactions, and scalability constraints.
