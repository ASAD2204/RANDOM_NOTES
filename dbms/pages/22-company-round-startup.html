Startup-Style DBMS Interview

Pragmatic schema/query design and rapid trade-off discussion.

▶ Typical asks

  • Quick schema for MVP product
  • Write essential API-supporting queries
  • Plan index growth as data scales
  • Suggest migration/partition strategy at scale

🌟 Winning Approach

  • State assumptions explicitly.
  • Deliver working baseline first, then optimize.
  • Balance normalization and practical performance.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Database engineering decisions should connect schema design, query behavior, indexing, transactions, and scalability constraints.
