Revision Chunk C (11-14)

Procedures/triggers, optimization, common interview SQL, and practice strategy.

📚 10-Minute Recall

  • Stored procedures encapsulate reusable logic in DB.
  • Triggers automate event-driven actions; use carefully.
  • Optimization starts with EXPLAIN and index-aware query design.
  • Core interview SQL patterns: ranking, duplicates, anti-joins.
  • Practice should be timed and output-verified.

▶ Rapid Drill

  1. Second highest salary in two ways.
  2. Nth highest salary with window function.
  3. Find duplicates by email.
  4. Employees without departments.
  5. Top salary per department.

🌟 Interview Tip

For SQL answers, include one optimized variant and discuss why it scales better.

▶ Theory Deep Dive

Database engineering decisions should connect schema design, query behavior, indexing, transactions, and scalability constraints.
